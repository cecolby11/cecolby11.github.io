About

Learning to write good code, learn/problem-solve in any language like a programmer, and develop web applications with JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Node.js in Northwestern University's full-stack coding bootcamp.

Casey recieved her B.A. in Economics and Linguistics from Northwestern University in June 2015. She is the lab manager and software developer for Northwestern University's Child Language Development Lab, where she discovered her passion for web development and programming by virtue of developing mobile applications to extend the Child Lab's experimental capabilities with children.
